Privacy Policy for Mishka

Last Updated: 10th August 2024

Say hi to Mishka, a Discord bot designed for interactive commands on Discord. This Privacy Policy outlines how Mishka ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide while using the bot. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

1. Information Collected

Mishka does not store any personal user data, messages, or content. The bot only processes data necessary for its functionality, including server and user IDs, message content (for command processing), and necessary permissions to perform actions in Discord servers.

2. Use of Information

Mishka uses the collected information solely for the purpose of providing its intended services, including but not limited to changing nicknames, reading messages, sending messages, Mishkaging messages, and utilizing other listed features. We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your information with third parties.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected by Mishka. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the security of user information.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Mishka does not use cookies or any tracking mechanisms to collect additional information beyond what is necessary for its functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

Mishka may utilize third-party services or APIs for specific features. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of these third-party services independently, as Mishka is not responsible for their practices.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time without notice. Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the beginning of the document.

7. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us @notvane through Discord. By using Mishka, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
